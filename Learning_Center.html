Glossary of Common Tech or Online Safety Terms

Phishing Fake emails, texts, or calls pretending to be from banks, government, or stores to steal info.
Malware Harmful software that enters your phone or computer and steals or destroys data.
2FA Extra code sent to your phone to make your login more secure.
VPN Protects your online activity and hides your location.
OTP (One-Time Password) A short code sent to your phone that is used only once to verify your identity.
Spam Text / Spam Email Unwanted messages containing ads or possible scams.
Scam Link A fake website link that looks real but is designed to steal information or money.
Online Wallet (e-wallet) An app like GCash or Maya used to send/receive money or pay bills.
QR Code A square “barcode” you scan with your phone to pay or open a website.
Fake Caller ID / Spoofing When scammers make their number look like it’s from a bank, office, or relative.
Two-Step Verification Another name for 2FA — using a second step (like a code) to secure your account.
Secure Website (HTTPS) A safe website with a lock icon beside the website address.
